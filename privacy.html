Last updated: May 2026

Mallo ("the App") is developed by PeterConway314 as a free tool. This page is used to inform users regarding the policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use this Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

Mallo is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2. Log Data & Analytics

To improve the quality of the service and fix bugs, Mallo uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device type and app usage patterns. This data is fully anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

The app uses third-party services from Google:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Used to monitor general user retention.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to send crash reports when the app crashes.

3. Local Data Storage

Mallo stores your "Pause Counts" and app settings locally on your device. This data remains on your phone and is not uploaded anywhere.
